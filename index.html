Who We Are

More Than Books —
A Learning Community

At Fun Learning Academy, we believe every child deserves tools that make education joyful, meaningful, and effective. We craft resources that speak to young minds through colour, story, play, and discovery.

Founded by Sami — born from a simple but powerful belief: that the right book, at the right moment, can change a child's entire relationship with learning.

Our team of educators, illustrators, and child development specialists collaborate on every product. Proudly rooted in Zimbabwe, building resources that reflect our children and our world.

Why Choose Us

Built Around Your
Child's Growth

We don't just make products — we make promises. Every book is crafted with your child's development at the very centre.

Premium Quality Design

Every product is beautifully illustrated and printed on high-quality, child-safe materials. We believe learning resources should feel as good as they work — because they deserve the best.

Child Development Focus

Developed with educators and child psychologists, our resources support cognitive, social, and emotional milestones at every age stage — aligned with both school and home learning.

Trusted by Thousands

With over 5,000 families across Zimbabwe trusting our brand, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of safety, accuracy, and educational integrity in every single product.
