Who We Are

More Than Books — A Learning Community

At Fun Learning Academy, we believe every child deserves tools that make education joyful, meaningful, and effective. We craft resources that speak to young minds in their own language — through colour, story, play, and discovery.

Founded by Sami, Fun Learning Academy was born from a simple but powerful belief: that the right book, at the right moment, can change a child's entire relationship with learning.

Our team of educators, illustrators, and child development specialists collaborate on every product — ensuring not just that children enjoy them, but that they genuinely grow from them. We are proudly rooted in Zimbabwe, building resources that reflect our children and our world.
